About Monet

Oscar-Claude Monet was a French painter born in 1840. He is known as the founder of impressionist painting and recognized for his paintings of nature and the world around him. Although he died in 1926, he is still seen as one of the most famous Western painters of all time. You can find his paintings displayed in multiple museums across the world, including the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York and the Louvre Museum in Paris, France.
